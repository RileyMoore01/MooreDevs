Our Story

Welcome to La Cabana, where the authentic flavors of Guanajuato come alive in the heart of Lubbock, Texas. For years, we've been serving our community with homemade corn tortillas, fresh salsas, and traditional Mexican recipes that have been passed down through generations.

Our commitment to authenticity means we prepare everything from scratch daily. From our perfectly seasoned carne guisada to our tender barbacoa, every dish reflects the true taste of Mexican home cooking.

Step into our warm, family-friendly atmosphere where Tejano music sets the mood and our attentive staff treats you like family. Whether you're joining us for breakfast or dinner, you'll experience the kind of hospitality that keeps our community coming back.
